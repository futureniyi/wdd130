Exciting Expeditions

Our mission is to provide the most thrilling and safe white water rafting experiences for adventure seekers of all ages. Join us on the river!

Since 1990, Exciting Expeditions has grown from one raft and a bold vision into a trusted name in river adventure. What began as weekend runs is now a full-service experience for thrill-seekers of all ages.

History

With decades on the water, we’re proud to deliver safe, unforgettable trips that bring people together—one rapid at a time. Join in spending more exciting decades in water. We are historically awesome, but we want to write more stories.
