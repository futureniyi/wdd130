Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to know how to swim to join a trip?

Nope! All participants wear coast guard-approved life jackets, and our guides are trained in swift-water rescue.

What should I wear for rafting?

Quick-drying clothes, water shoes or sandals with a heel strap, and sunscreen. Avoid cotton and flip-flops.

Are there age or health restrictions?

Most trips are suitable for ages 5 and up. Participants should be in reasonable health and able to follow instructions.

How do group bookings work?

You can book as a group in the form below. Group discounts and custom time slots are available—just ask!

What happens if it rains?

Rain makes rafting more fun! We run trips in most weather conditions, but may reschedule if safety is a concern.

Can I bring a camera or phone?

Only if it's waterproof and secured! We also offer action shots taken by our river photographers.
